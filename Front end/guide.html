Guide

How does anonyMate work? All you have to do is follow these simple steps:
1. Sign up with the registered admission number and a pin provided by your school.
2. Upon signing up, you will be asked to change your pin so as to keep your identity confidential.
3. When you require help you can freely sign in using the admission number and pin generated by you during sign up process.
4. Video Call sessions can be availed over zoom.
