Thrive

Students tackle with self-doubts and fear of failure from time to time. Counselling will help you to tame your inner critic and break patterns of self-defeating thoughts and limiting beliefs. Mental-health issues seem to be on the rise. Stress, bullying, suicidal thoughts are common problems faced by students these days. We at anonyMate aim to solve all the problems faced by them by providing student a platform where they can easily register and communicate with their school counsellor without worrying about their confidentiality.
